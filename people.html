Contact

I am Remko Duursma, Data Scientist, R Trainer & Consultant. After a career in science, I now focus on practical applications in data science, and offering trainings on a variety of topics.

I have organized, taught, and prepared all course materials for many often highy customized R workshops. During the period 2012 – 2018 I organized a popular R course at Western Sydney University, which generated a lot of interest outside the university. Next to my employment as a data scientist I now teach R courses - from beginner to advanced.

I bring the following expertise to the workshops and courses at DataPelikaan:

  • Project Management In R. I have a lot of experience in organizing, structuring, and maintaining large, complex data projects in R. The challenge is to set up transparent, repeatable and robust projects – much more than just an R script. I have mastered many techniques - some my own - to improve reliability and transparency of complex R projects.
  • Interactive Data-driven Web Applications with R/Shiny. I am highly skilled in developing interactive web applications, bringing together data, visualizations, reporting, and automating tasks.
  • R Packages. I have developed more than 10 R packages that make complex, often specialized analyses more accessible. Developing your own R packages is an invaluable tool to help organize complex workflows, and to generate robust testable code.
I am a native Dutch speaker, and a fluent English speaker
